## What is Nexus Hotel?

Nexus Hotel is a **digital reservation platform** designed to provide current and new customers with a **fast, secure, and reliable** way to book hotel facilities, eliminating the dependency on traditional phone-based reservation methods.

## The Business Problem

The current phone-based reservation system at Nexus Hotel presents several critical challenges:

<Warning>
  **Security Risks**: Handling personal information, contact details, accommodation preferences, and banking data through phone calls creates significant cybersecurity vulnerabilities.
</Warning>

### Key Pain Points

- **Inefficient**: Manual phone-based process is time-consuming and error-prone
- **Slow**: Limited by reception staff availability and working hours
- **Vulnerable**: Exposes sensitive customer data to security risks
- **Unscalable**: Cannot handle high-volume periods effectively

The platform handles sensitive information including:
- Personal and contact information
- Accommodation preferences
- Banking and payment data

This makes it essential to guarantee **high levels of security, availability, and reliability**.

## Why DevOps Methodology?

<Info>
  From the perspective of business leaders, politicians, and everyday customers, a critical question emerges: **Is it safe for anyone with basic IT knowledge to access personal data, booking dates, and times?**
</Info>

Customers planning special events need to feel confident when sharing **banking data**, trusting that the hotel implements both minimum and advanced **cybersecurity measures**.

The **DevOps methodology** ensures a continuous improvement cycle, reinforcing both the **security** and **stability** of the system.

### Key Benefits of DevOps

<Accordion title="Continuous Security">
  Integration of automated testing and vulnerability analysis within the development cycle to detect and fix issues before they impact customers.
  
  - Automated security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
  - Early detection of vulnerabilities
  - Rapid patching and updates
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="High Service Availability">
  Fast and controlled updates without interruptions, ensuring the reservation system is always online.
  
  - Zero-downtime deployments
  - Automated rollbacks on failures
  - 24/7 system availability
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Open Source Collaboration">
  Process standardization through version control, continuous integration, and shared repositories, ensuring quality and avoiding conflicts.
  
  - Git-based version control
  - Code review processes
  - Collaborative development workflows
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Scalability and Performance">
  Ability to monitor the system in real-time and automatically scale resources during high-demand periods.
  
  - Real-time monitoring with Grafana
  - Auto-scaling infrastructure
  - Performance optimization
</Accordion>
